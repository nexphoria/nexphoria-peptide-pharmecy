01 — Brand Overview

Futuristic Luxury Lab

Nexphoria presents research-grade peptides through the lens of precision science and sober luxury. Every design decision reinforces expertise, restraint, and trust.

Brand voice in one sentence: Clinical, educational, precise — sober luxury, not hype. Products are presented as research compounds; copy never promises personal health outcomes or implies approved therapeutic use.

Brand Pillars

Precision

Every spec, purity %, and storage condition is stated with exactness. No rounding, no vagueness.

Restraint

Whitespace is an asset. Fewer elements, more intention. Luxury communicates through what is absent as much as what is present.

Education

Informed customers are empowered customers. Every product page teaches; the brand earns trust through knowledge.

Aspiration

"Find Your Focus" — aspirational storytelling belongs on brand/about pages only. Never as product claims.

Logo Usage Rules

The Nexphoria wordmark and typemark are the primary brand identifiers. Use the approved files from the official logo library — never recreate, approximate, or retype the logo.

Clear Zone

Always maintain a minimum clear zone equal to the cap-height of the wordmark on all four sides. No other graphic elements, text, or imagery may intrude into this zone.

NEXPHORIA

Dashed border indicates minimum clear zone — approximately the cap-height of the wordmark.

Minimum Size

NEXPHORIA
Digital min: 80px wide
NEXPHORIA
Too small — avoid

Print minimum: 25mm wide. Digital minimum: 80px wide. Below these sizes, legibility of secondary details is compromised.

Do
  • Use only approved SVG/PNG master files
  • Place on approved background colours (cream, near-black, olive)
  • Maintain clear zone on all sides
  • Use the tymemark alone on brand merchandise or favicon contexts
  • Scale proportionally
Don't
  • Recreate or retype the wordmark in any font
  • Stretch, skew, or rotate the logo
  • Add drop shadows, outlines, or effects
  • Place on busy photographic backgrounds without an overlay
  • Change the logo colour arbitrarily
  • Place below minimum size

06 — Visual Motifs

Approved Visual Motifs

Visual motifs are recurring graphic elements that reinforce the brand's scientific-luxury positioning. Use each motif as specified — overuse or misuse dilutes the premium feel.

Chemical / Molecular Pattern
The DNA/molecular line-work overlay. Use on dark hero backgrounds and section dividers. Scale at low opacity (5–15%). Never use at full opacity or over busy images.
Grain overlay (6% opacity)
Film Grain Texture
SVG fractalNoise overlay applied globally at 6% opacity via body::before. Adds warmth and tactility to what would otherwise be flat digital surfaces.
SVG Pharmaceutical Vial
Custom SVG vial illustrations replace product photography on cards and the product detail page. Each vial inherits the product category accent color. See src/components/ProductVial.tsx.

Focus
Dark Gradient Panels
Section backgrounds alternating between light cream and dark near-black. Dark panels use subtle radial gold gradient at 8–12% opacity. Never flat black — always warm dark tones.
Lot #NEX-2024-0441 99.1% purity
Hairline Rules
1px horizontal rules in #E5E5E5 (light) or #2A2A28 (dark) used to separate data rows, section zones, and spec tables. Precision detail that echoes lab documentation.
Grid / Lab Paper Overlay
Ultra-subtle grid pattern used in select section backgrounds (e.g., science page) to evoke laboratory graph paper. Maximum 5% opacity. Use only on light backgrounds.

Photography Art Direction

Approved image styles
  • Clinical lab environments: clean benches, glassware, precision equipment
  • Close-up vial and syringe photography with clinical lighting
  • High-contrast black-and-white lab imagery
  • Desaturated, cool-toned scientific imagery
  • Abstract molecular / structural close-ups
Avoid
  • Fitness / gym / bodybuilding imagery
  • Celebrity or influencer-style photography
  • Highly saturated or warm-toned lifestyle images
  • Images showing product use on humans
  • Competitive product comparisons
