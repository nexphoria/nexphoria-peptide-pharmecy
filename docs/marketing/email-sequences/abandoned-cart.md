You still have items in your cart. Before you finalize your sourcing decision — with us or anyone else — here's something worth understanding.

**Not all purity claims are equal.**

"≥98% purity" from one supplier and "≥99% purity" from another might sound similar. The difference is in *how* that purity was determined and *what* it accounts for.

HPLC measures the target compound as a percentage of total sample area under the curve. A result of ≥99% means less than 1% of the sample is anything other than the intended compound. But that 1% matters — especially across multiple experiments or compounding doses in animal models.
