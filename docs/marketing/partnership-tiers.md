# Nexphoria Partnership Tiers & Program Structure

## Overview
Nexphoria partnership architecture built on four tiers, each optimized for different influencer profiles, audience sizes, and revenue models. Each tier includes dedicated UTM tracking, performance metrics, and escalation paths to higher-tier partnerships.

---

## TIER 1: Free Sample Program

**Target Profile:**
- Micro-influencers (5K-50K followers)
- Emerging peptide reviewers/testers
- Qualified practitioners with social presence
- Content creators with high engagement (even if smaller reach)
- First-time partnership opportunities to build relationship

**Program Details:**

### Who Qualifies
- Minimum 5K verified followers OR 15%+ engagement rate (even with smaller following)
- Documented history of product testing/review content
- Audience alignment with peptide/biohacking/fitness/longevity niches
- Willingness to provide honest evaluation (negative feedback acceptable)
- Valid mailing address for sample shipment

### What They Get
- Complete free 30-day protocol (full core peptide stack)
- Third-party COA documentation for each compound
- Research citations and mechanism summaries
- Direct line to Nexphoria formulation team for questions/adjustments
- 25% affiliate commission if their community purchases (optional but incentivized)
- Recognition on Nexphoria "tested by" creator section of website
- First opportunity to upgrade to Affiliate or Ambassador tier

### What We Ask in Return
- Honest personal evaluation (minimum 14-day usage before any feedback)
- Feedback form completion (3-5 questions on experience, absorption, effects)
- 6-month exclusivity on competitor peptide partnerships (non-restrictive)
- Optional content: Review/testimonial (not required, but strong incentive for continued samples)
- Affiliate link sharing if they recommend (no content obligation)
- Quarterly check-in on usage/results

### Tracking & Attribution

**UTM Structure:**
```
https://nexphoria.com/order?utm_source=influencer&utm_medium=affiliate&utm_campaign=free_sample&utm_content=[CREATOR_USERNAME]
```

**Affiliate Link Format:**
```
nexphoria.com/aff/[CREATOR_USERNAME]
```

**Dashboard Metrics:**
- Sample shipment date and delivery confirmation
- Evaluation completion status
- Feedback submission date/content
- Affiliate link click-through rate (CTR)
- Conversion rate from affiliate link
- AOV (Average Order Value) from affiliate purchases
- 30/60/90-day repurchase rate

### Sample Budget & Logistics
- Cost per sample pack: ~$45 COGS
- Typical monthly allocation: 20-30 sample packs
- Shipping: Covered by Nexphoria (USPS Priority)
- Re-supply: Available if initial feedback is positive
- Withdrawal window: Can stop program after 90 days if ROI insufficient

**Success Threshold:**
- Minimum 3 affiliate purchases within 90 days OR
- Minimum 5 content pieces mentioning Nexphoria OR
- Minimum 250 total clicks on affiliate link

## TIER 2: Affiliate Program

**Target Profile:**
- Established micro to mid-tier influencers (15K-200K followers)
- Creators with proven purchase intent audience
- Long-term partnership potential
- Consistent content creators in health/optimization space

**Program Details:**

### Commission Structure
- **Base Commission:** 25% of each referred sale
- **Tiered Bonus:** Additional 5% commission bonus at monthly thresholds
  - $500+ in monthly referral revenue: +5% (30% total)
  - $1,500+ in monthly referral revenue: +10% (35% total)
  - $3,000+ in monthly referral revenue: +15% (40% total)
  - **Cap:** 40% maximum commission per tier

### Cookie Duration
- **30-day attribution window**
- Purchase must occur within 30 days of click for commission
- Repeat purchases from same customer credited to original referrer for 12 months (unless new click)
- Cross-device tracking enabled (same email = same customer)

### Payment Terms
- Monthly reconciliation (1st of following month)
- Minimum $50 threshold to process payment
- ACH transfer or PayPal (creator's choice)
- Detailed monthly reporting with click-through, conversion, revenue breakdown

### Marketing Support Provided
- Branded pre-written social captions (5-10 variations per month)
- Product photography/lifestyle images (provided as downloadable pack)
- Sample educational posts on peptide mechanisms
- Monthly affiliate performance dashboard (real-time)
- Exclusive affiliate discount code (20% off, use code: [CREATOR]20)
- Early access to new product launches
- Quarterly strategy calls with Nexphoria partnership team

### What We Ask in Return
- Minimum 2 content pieces per month mentioning Nexphoria
- Content must include affiliate link
- Honest product recommendations (negative feedback redirected privately, not in public content)
- No exclusive brand deals with competitor peptide companies (12-month non-compete)
- Monthly check-in on affiliate performance/strategy
- Feedback on audience response to products

### Escalation Path
- Consistent $1,000+/month referral revenue for 3+ months → Ambassador tier consideration
- High-quality audience and engagement → Brand partnership negotiation
- Creator initiative to develop co-branded content → Wholesale/co-brand tier

### Tracking & Attribution

**UTM Structure (Standard):**
```
https://nexphoria.com/order?utm_source=influencer&utm_medium=affiliate&utm_campaign=general&utm_content=[CREATOR_USERNAME]&aff=[CREATOR_ID]
```

**Discount Code UTM:**
```
https://nexphoria.com/order?utm_source=influencer&utm_medium=discount&utm_campaign=[CREATOR]20&utm_content=[CREATOR_USERNAME]
```

**Dashboard Metrics:**
- Real-time click tracking
- Conversion rate per source
- Revenue attribution by creator
- Customer lifetime value (CLV) by referrer
- Churn rate of referred customers
- Repeat purchase rate
- AOV variance between referrers
- Conversion funnel analysis

**Fraud Prevention:**
- Monthly manual review of unusual click/conversion patterns
- IP clustering detection for bot traffic
- Customer feedback survey (asked if they know affiliate creator)
- Suspension clause: Immediate suspension for click fraud, artificial traffic, or misleading promotion

## TIER 3: Brand Ambassador Program

**Target Profile:**
- Established mid-to-large influencers (50K-500K followers)
- Creators with highly engaged, qualified audiences
- Regular content creators in health/longevity/fitness niches
- Potential for long-term partnership (6-24 months minimum)

**Program Details:**

### Monthly Supply & Compensation

**Base Package ($1,500-3,000/month, tier-dependent):**
- Unlimited monthly peptide supply (core product line)
- Exclusive custom formulations developed for ambassador
- Priority fulfillment and expedited shipping
- Brand ambassador exclusive discount code (40% off core products for audience)
- Payment: Monthly retainer + commission on discount code usage

**Tiered Compensation Structure:**
- **Tier A (50K-150K followers):** $1,500/month base + 30% commission on discount code sales
- **Tier B (150K-500K followers):** $2,500/month base + 35% commission on discount code sales
- **Tier C (500K+ followers):** $3,500/month base + 40% commission on discount code sales

### Exclusive Discount Code Structure

**Format:** `[CREATOR_NAME_CODE]` (e.g., JOEBIOHACK)
- **Audience Discount:** 40% off all Nexphoria products
- **Non-expiring:** Code active for 12-month partnership duration
- **Tracked Separately:** All discount code purchases attributed to ambassador
- **Revenue Share:** Monthly reporting on discount code usage + commission payout

**UTM Structure:**
```
https://nexphoria.com/order?utm_source=influencer&utm_medium=ambassador&utm_campaign=[CREATOR_CODE]&utm_content=[CREATOR_USERNAME]&code=[CREATOR_CODE]
```

### Content Commitments (Minimum)

**Monthly Deliverables:**
- 4 organic social posts (mix of Instagram/TikTok/YouTube Shorts)
- 1 long-form content piece (article, video, podcast feature—platform flexible)
- Weekly stories/updates featuring Nexphoria products
- Quarterly deeper educational content (20+ minute video, detailed article, or podcast episode)

**Content Rights:**
- Creator owns all content; Nexphoria can repost with attribution
- Nexphoria can feature ambassador content on website/marketing
- Creator retains full editorial control

### Exclusive Benefits

- **Early Product Access:** 30-day advance notice of new launches
- **Co-Development Opportunity:** Input on formulation development for new ambassador-exclusive products
- **Custom Formulation:** One annually custom peptide protocol developed specifically for ambassador's use case
- **Speaking Engagement Opportunities:** Invited to speak at Nexphoria events/webinars (paid separately if desired)
- **Educational Content Support:** Nexphoria provides research, citations, scientist access for content creation
- **Exclusive Community:** Private Slack channel with other Nexphoria ambassadors for collaboration
- **VIP Customer Support:** Direct line to Nexphoria for audience support questions

### Performance Metrics & Benchmarks

**Minimum Performance Standards (quarterly review):**
- Average post engagement rate ≥ 3% (TikTok/Instagram) or ≥ 1% (YouTube)
- Minimum 3 of 4 monthly posts delivered on schedule
- Discount code usage generating ≥ $2,000 in attributed revenue (tier-dependent)
- Audience sentiment toward content positive (assessed via comments, DMs, feedback)
- No public criticism or competitive brand partnerships

**Escalation Triggers for Termination:**
- Consistent miss on content deliverables
- Discount code revenue < $500/month for 2+ consecutive months
- Negative sentiment/audience feedback about Nexphoria partnership
- Engagement rate drops >50% from baseline
- Public announcement of competing peptide brand partnership

### Term & Renewal

- **Initial Term:** 12 months
- **Renewal Option:** Mutual agreement to renew at improved rates (typically +$500/month increase based on performance)
- **Early Termination Clause:** 30-day notice required; pro-rata refund not applicable (monthly compensation)
- **Non-Compete:** 6-month period post-partnership before endorsing competitor peptide brands

### Tracking & Dashboard

**Dedicated Ambassador Dashboard Includes:**
- Real-time discount code usage tracking
- Attribution revenue by post/content piece
- Audience growth metrics (followers, engagement trend)
- Content performance analytics (reach, engagement, saves)
- Commission accrual and payout schedule
- Custom cohort analysis (audience demographics, purchase behavior)
- Monthly performance scorecard

## TIER 4: Wholesale/Co-Brand Partnership

**Target Profile:**
- Large-reach influencers (200K+ followers) OR established practitioners (clinic owners, wellness centers)
- Creators with strong brand in health/optimization space
- Potential for revenue-share vs. affiliate-only model
- Interest in deeper brand integration and potential co-branded products

**Program Details:**

### Partnership Structure Options

**Option A: Co-Branded Product Line**
- Nexphoria + Creator branded peptide stack
- Custom formulation developed jointly
- Creator owns branding; Nexphoria handles manufacturing/fulfillment
- Revenue split: 50/50 net after COGS and fulfillment
- Minimum order commitment: 500 units (first batch)
- Wholesale price to creator: 35% of retail

**Option B: Wholesale Resale**
- Creator orders Nexphoria products at wholesale price
- Wholesale discount: 40-50% off retail (volume-dependent)
- Minimum first order: $5,000
- Reorder flexibility (no minimum order for subsequent purchases)
- Pricing tiers unlock at higher volume:
  - $5K-15K/month: 40% discount
  - $15K-50K/month: 45% discount
  - $50K+/month: 50% discount
- Creator markets under own brand/packaging (white label available)

**Option C: Revenue Share Model**
- Creator launches Nexphoria-branded online store for their audience
- Nexphoria provides inventory/fulfillment
- Revenue split: 60% creator / 40% Nexphoria (net after fulfillment costs)
- Minimum monthly revenue guarantee: $2,000 (first 6 months, then reassessed)
- Promotional support from both parties

### Qualification Requirements

**For Influencers:**
- Minimum 200K verified followers
- Documented consistent sales/purchase intent audience
- 6+ months of prior partnership success with Nexphoria (or similar brands)
- Audience demographic alignment with Nexphoria target market

**For Practitioners:**
- Established clinic/practice with 500+ active patient base
- Licensed healthcare practitioner (MD, DO, NP, PA, or similar—varies by state)
- Current or prior experience recommending peptide therapies
- Professional reputation check (background verification)

### Marketing & Support

- **Co-Branded Marketing Assets:** Shared marketing materials, campaign planning
- **Lead Generation:** Nexphoria can support audience-building for creator's store
- **Customer Success:** Priority support for wholesale customer inquiries
- **Content Collaboration:** Joint educational content, webinar series
- **Event Opportunities:** Potential speaking slots at industry events
- **Research Access:** Early data on product performance in creator's audience

### Financial Terms

**Invoicing & Payment:**
- Net 30 payment terms
- Monthly reconciliation (sales volume, revenue share)
- ACH or wire transfer
- Detailed itemized reporting

**Minimum Volume Commitments (Option C):**
- Month 1-3: $2,000 minimum monthly revenue
- Month 4-12: $5,000 minimum monthly revenue
- Year 2+: $10,000 minimum monthly revenue (or renegotiate terms)

**Breach Clause:**
- Two consecutive months below minimum: Renegotiate or terminate with 60-day notice
- Non-performance termination: 90-day phase-out period

### Tracking & Attribution

**Wholesale-Level UTM Structure:**
```
https://nexphoria.com/order?utm_source=wholesale&utm_medium=[PARTNER_NAME]&utm_campaign=[PARTNER_STORE]&utm_content=wholesale
```

**Creator Store Tracking (Option C):**
- Dedicated store subdomain: `[creator].nexphoria.com`
- All transactions tracked to creator's account
- Real-time dashboard with sales, revenue, customer data
- Export capabilities for accounting/tax purposes

**Dashboard Metrics:**
- Daily/weekly/monthly sales volume
- Revenue by product line
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Customer lifetime value (CLV)
- Churn rate of created customer cohort
- Repeat purchase rate
- NPS (Net Promoter Score) of co-branded product

### Co-Branding Specifics

**For Co-Branded Products:**
- Joint naming approval (examples: "Nexphoria + Creator Longevity Stack")
- Packaging design collaboration (creator input required)
- Marketing asset usage: shared ownership, mutual promotion
- Intellectual property: Formulation owned by Nexphoria; branding owned jointly
- Trademark registration: Nexphoria handles; cost split 50/50
- Shelf life & quality: Nexphoria manufacturing standards

**Launch Timeline:**
- Development & formulation: 6-8 weeks
- Packaging & production: 4-6 weeks
- Marketing launch: 2-week lead-up campaign
- First batch fulfillment: 60-90 days from partnership signing

### Term & Exit

- **Initial Term:** 24 months
- **Renewal:** Automatic renewal unless 90-day termination notice provided
- **Performance Review:** Annual review of sales targets and partnership satisfaction
- **IP Rights Post-Termination:** Creator can continue selling co-branded product for 6 months at same wholesale rate; after 6 months, transitions to standard wholesale pricing or discontinuation

## Cross-Tier Escalation Path

```
Free Sample Program
        ↓ (Positive evaluation + audience engagement)
        ↓
Affiliate Program (15K+ followers or high engagement)
        ↓ (Consistent $1,000+/month affiliate revenue)
        ↓
Brand Ambassador ($1,500-3,000/month retainer + commission)
        ↓ (Proven sales impact, large audience, strategic fit)
        ↓
Wholesale/Co-Brand Partnership ($5K+/month volume or revenue share)
```

**Dual-Track Path:**
- Creator can participate in Affiliate + Ambassador simultaneously
- Practitioners can move directly from Free Sample → Wholesale (if clinic-based)
- High-performing ambassadors can launch co-branded product line while maintaining base ambassador agreement

## Universal Program Terms

### Compliance & Legal

**All Tiers Must Comply With:**
- FTC Guidelines on influencer disclosures (#ad, #sponsored, clear ad labeling)
- FDA regulations on supplement/peptide promotion (no disease claims)
- State medical practice laws (practitioners cannot prescribe; Nexphoria handles that barrier)
- Brand safety standards (no association with illegal substance promotion, misinformation)

**Content Review Process:**
- Free Sample: No pre-approval required
- Affiliate: No pre-approval (spot checks for compliance)
- Ambassador: Pre-approval of long-form educational content (1-week turnaround)
- Wholesale: Marketing materials reviewed for compliance (2-week turnaround)

### Dispute Resolution

- 30-day notice for any partnership termination (standard tier-dependent terms apply)
- 60-day mediation period before legal action
- Arbitration clause (binding): Any disputes resolved via JAMS arbitration, not litigation
- Costs split evenly unless clear breach established

### Non-Disparagement

- Partner agrees not to publicly disparage Nexphoria or its products
- Private feedback channels available for concerns
- Breach of non-disparagement = immediate partnership termination

### Data Privacy

- Partner data (emails, metrics) held confidential
- No third-party sharing without written consent
- Opt-out available for marketing communications
- GDPR/CCPA compliant data handling
