## 🔴 Competitor Weaknesses (Bullets)

### ❌ Peptide Sciences (SHUT DOWN — March 2026)
- Voluntarily closed; entire customer base (~70K+ weekly visitors) now shopping for alternatives
- No rebrand or successor announced — **massive market gap**
- Was strongest SEO player; their domain authority is now decaying
- **Nexphoria opportunity:** Target "Peptide Sciences alternative" keywords NOW

---

### ❌ Blue Sky Peptide
- Only sells 5mg vials by default — forces customers to buy 2× for 10mg dose
- Dated website UX; checkout feels 2015-era
- No subscription / auto-ship option
- Very limited educational content; no blog strategy
- **No 10mg single-vial option** = customer frustration

---

### ❌ Limitless Life Nootropics (aka Limitless Biotech)
- Brand identity confusion (changed name from "Limitless Life" to "Limitless Biotech")
- Customer trust issues from rebranding
- Inconsistent stock availability on popular SKUs
- Capsule product claims are vague; weak COA presentation
- Shipping delays reported in community forums

---

### ❌ Amino Asylum
- **Cheapest on market but COA authenticity is questioned** — Reddit users flagged Janoshik COAs with missing verification codes
- Primarily SARMs-focused; peptide line feels like an afterthought
- Spray formats cause confusion around dosing accuracy
- No dedicated cold-chain or lyophilization info
- Website domain fragmentation (multiple URLs: aminoasylum.us, amino-asylum.net, aminoasylum-llc.com)

---

### ❌ Core Peptides
- Heavy reliance on Yahoo Finance press releases as "earned media" — perceived as fake PR
- Limited product variety; small catalog compared to category leaders
- Thin social presence; almost zero community engagement
- No loyalty/subscription program
- Website design is generic and forgettable

---

### ❌ Behemoth Labz
- Primarily a SARMs vendor; peptides feel secondary
- Brand name ("Behemoth") doesn't align with research-grade positioning
- Payment infrastructure issues reported (crypto-only complaints)
- Limited customer education content
- No cold-chain shipping guarantee messaging

---

### ❌ Swiss Chems
- **NOT US-based** — international fulfillment means slower shipping + customs risk
- Capsule format BPC-157 is controversial (bioavailability debates)
- Checkout requires crypto for many payment methods
- Expensive: BPC+TB blend = $132.95/10mg (highest priced)
- Regulatory risk from EU jurisdiction vs. US customers

---

### ❌ Biotech Peptides
- **Free shipping only on orders over $200** — barrier for new customers
- Limited brand voice; very clinical/sterile tone
- No community or forum presence
- Blog content is sparse and rarely updated
- No loyalty rewards or referral program
