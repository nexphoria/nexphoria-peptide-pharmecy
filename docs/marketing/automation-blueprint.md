## 🤖 THE MACHINE (What runs without you)

### Email Automation (Klaviyo — FREE)
```
New visitor → Popup → Email captured
  → Email 1 (instant): "Welcome to Nexphoria" + brand story
  → Email 2 (+2 days): "How we test every batch" + COA explainer
  → Email 3 (+4 days): Research guide PDF (BPC-157 or NAD+)
  → Email 4 (+7 days): Social proof + top compounds
  → Email 5 (+10 days): Soft offer — "Your first order"

Cart abandoned →
  → Email 1 (+1 hr): "Still researching?"
  → Email 2 (+24 hr): Educational content about product
  → Email 3 (+72 hr): Last chance

Post purchase →
  → Email 1 (instant): Order confirm + storage guide
  → Email 2 (+7 days): Research tips for their compound
  → Email 3 (+21 days): Reorder prompt + complementary product
```

## WHAT CHIYA DOES (30 min/day max)
1. **Morning (5 min):** Read Atlas brief, approve/deny pending actions
2. **Reddit (10 min):** Post Atlas-drafted answers in 2-3 subreddits
3. **Review (5 min):** Check Shopify dashboard — orders, revenue
4. **Decision (10 min):** Any approvals needed (influencer shipments, etc.)

**Everything else: Atlas.**
