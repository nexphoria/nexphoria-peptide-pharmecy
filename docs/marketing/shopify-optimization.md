### How to Implement Product Page SEO in Shopify

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Log into Shopify Admin**
   - Go to: `https://y7v2we-sn.myshopify.com/admin`
   - Click: **Products** (left sidebar)

2. **Edit Product Meta Title & Description**
   - Click on the product name
   - Scroll down to: **Search engine listing preview**
   - Click: **Edit website SEO**
   - In **Page title:** Paste the meta title from above
   - In **Meta description:** Paste the meta description (max 155 chars)
   - Ensure **URL handle** matches the format: `/products/compound-name-lowercase`
   - Click: **Save**

3. **Add H1 Tag (in Product Description)**
   - In the **Description** field, start with the H1 tag as the first heading:
     ```
     # [Compound Name] — Research-Grade Peptide | ≥99% HPLC | US Synthesis
     ```
   - Include the product details below

4. **Optimize Product Description Structure**
   - **Section 1:** Opening + What It Is (2-3 sentences)
   - **Section 2:** Published Research Applications (bullet points)
   - **Section 3:** Our Quality Standards (HPLC + LAL testing callout)
   - **Section 4:** Storage & Reconstitution (if applicable)
   - **Section 5:** Certificate of Analysis (download link + CTA)
   - **Final CTA:** "Add to Cart" or "Get Pricing"

5. **Internal Linking Strategy**
   - Link to related products: e.g., from BPC-157, link to TB-500, CJC-1295, and Performance Stack
   - Link to blog articles: e.g., from Semaglutide, link to `/blogs/research/semaglutide-research-peptide`
   - Link to Collection pages: e.g., "Browse all Metabolic Research Compounds →"

6. **Rich Snippets & Schema Markup**
   - Shopify automatically applies Product schema
   - Ensure: Product name, price, availability, and review count are accurate
   - Add image alt text: `[Compound Name] research peptide - ≥99% HPLC verified by Nexphoria`

### How to Implement Collection Page SEO in Shopify

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Log into Shopify Admin**
   - Click: **Products → Collections**

2. **Create or Edit Collection**
   - Click: **Create collection** (or click existing collection name to edit)
   - Enter **Collection title:** Use the collection name (e.g., "Recovery Peptides")
   - Set **Collection type:** Automated (optional, based on product tags)

3. **Add Collection Meta Title & Description**
   - Scroll down to: **Search engine listing preview**
   - Click: **Edit website SEO**
   - **Page title:** Paste the meta title
   - **Meta description:** Paste the meta description
   - **URL handle:** Confirm it matches format `/collections/recovery-peptides`
   - Click: **Save**

4. **Add Collection Description**
   - In the **Description** field (rich text editor), paste the collection description
   - Start with: `# [Collection Name]` (H1 tag)
   - Include bullets, links to related products and blog posts
   - Click: **Save**

5. **Organize Products within Collection**
   - Click: **Products** section
   - Click: **Browse** and add relevant products
   - Reorder by dragging if needed
   - Click: **Save**

6. **Optimize Filters & Sorting**
   - Add product tags to enable filtering (e.g., "recovery", "performance", "longevity")
   - Ensure default sort is by relevance or best sellers

## SHOPIFY EMAIL AUTOMATION SETUP {#shopify-email-automation}

Shopify has native email marketing built into the admin dashboard. Below is the step-by-step setup for four key automation flows.

### IMPORTANT: Shopify Email vs. Klaviyo
Shopify Email is included in most plans (free, with per-email costs). This guide uses native Shopify Email for simplicity. For advanced segmentation and deliverability, you can later migrate to Klaviyo.

---

### AUTOMATION 1: WELCOME EMAIL FLOW

**Trigger:** Customer subscribes to email list (via popup or footer signup)  
**Flow:** 5 emails over 10 days

#### Setup Instructions:

1. **Enable Shopify Email**
   - In Shopify Admin, click: **Settings → Apps and integrations**
   - Search: "Shopify Email"
   - Click: **Install** (if not already installed)

2. **Create Welcome Automation**
   - Click: **Customers → Email marketing → Automations**
   - Click: **Create automation**
   - Select trigger: **Customer subscribes to marketing emails**
   - Click: **Next**

3. **Set Up Email 1 (Welcome — Instant)**
   - **Scheduled:** Immediate
   - **Subject:** Welcome to Nexphoria
   - **Preview text:** US-synthesized. HPLC-verified. Research-ready.
   - **Email body:** Copy from `email-sequences/welcome-flow.md` → Email 1
   - Paste into Shopify's email editor
   - Click: **Save**

4. **Add Email 2 (HPLC & LAL Testing — +2 Days)**
   - Click: **Add another email**
   - **Scheduled:** 2 days after previous email
   - **Subject:** How we test every batch
   - **Preview text:** HPLC and LAL testing — what they measure and why it matters.
   - **Email body:** Copy from `email-sequences/welcome-flow.md` → Email 2
   - Click: **Save**

5. **Add Email 3 (Research Guide — +4 Days)**
   - Click: **Add another email**
   - **Scheduled:** 4 days after previous email

   - **Preview text:** BPC-157 and NAD+ — what the published literature says.

   - **Email body:** Copy from `email-sequences/welcome-flow.md` → Email 4

   - Click: **Add another email**
   - **Scheduled:** 10 days after previous email
   - **Subject:** Ready when you are
   - **Preview text:** No pressure. Your research, your timeline.
