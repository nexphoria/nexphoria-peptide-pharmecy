Key Research Differentiators
Sermorelin
Shortest t½ in class
Native GHRH fragment. Ideal for controlled GH pulse studies where precise timing and rapid clearance are required. FDA-approved clinical precedent.
CJC-1295
DAC = albumin binding
Maleimide-Cys34 covalent albumin bond extends t½ from minutes to days. Once-weekly protocol. Phase I/II trials documented 6–8 day GH/IGF-1 elevation per injection.
Ipamorelin
Cleanest selectivity
Pentapeptide GHS-R1a agonist. Only compound in class with confirmed absence of cortisol, prolactin, and ACTH co-secretion at therapeutic doses.
MK-677
Only oral option
Non-peptide small molecule. Oral bioavailability eliminates reconstitution. ~24h t½ enables consistent once-daily protocol with sustained IGF-1 elevation.
